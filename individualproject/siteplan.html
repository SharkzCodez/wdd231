Site plan for Better Bettas, a site for the care and history of betta fish

Zsa Zsa LeBlanc | WDD231

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to share the proper care of betta fish, as many pet stores give new betta owners misinformation that can harm or kill the fish.

Audience

The audience is new and soon-to-be betta fish owners as well as people who work at pet stores who want to change the cruel conditions betta fish are usually stuck in.

Scenarios

What size tank should I get for my betta?

What other fish can I keep with my betta?
